about us

Here at e-Saving Solutions, we find modern solutions for modern problems

Established in 2019 as a University based software development team, e-Saving Solutions has been working towards technological success, while maintaining an environmentally friendly outlook. Our aim is to provide efficient and effective solutions that tackle our clients problems in an energy saving manner that is beneficial to our clients, ourselves as a company, and the environment as a whole.
